Fomula behind the scene

First we have the mole fraction of feed flow \(x_f\), distillate flow \(x_d\) and bottom flow \(x_w\).

Each system has different vapor - liquid equilibrium (VLE) data so make sure you choose carefully
\(y_f\) can be interpolated from VLE data
\(R\) is the reflux ratio calculated through \(R_{min}\) as follow $$R_{min}=\frac{x_d - y_f}{y_f - x_f}$$ $$R = 1.3\ R_{min} + 0.3$$ \(y^*_f\) is the mole fraction of subtance in Operating line can be calculated below: $$y^*_f=\frac{R}{R+1}x_f + \frac{x_d}{R + 1}$$
And finally, we can have two line recitify and strpping easily
Recitify line determined with two point (\(x_f\), \(y^*_f\)) and (\(x_d\), \(y_d\)) where \(x_d = y_d\)
Stripping line determined with two point (\(x_f\), \(y^*_f\)) and (\(x_w\), \(y_w\)) where \(x_w = y_w\)
